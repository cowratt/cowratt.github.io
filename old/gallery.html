Knight

This is most ambitious project I've worked on so far. It was made in Blender and animated using motion capture data from a Microsoft Kinect. I started working on it in October of 2016 for an animation class, and finished it in the summer of 2017. It tells the story of a knight's journey through bizarre worlds in search of a shield.
