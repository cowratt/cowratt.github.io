I'm a guy that likes making things. I like digital art, I write code and make short videos, and occasionally I publish songs. This site is new and i haven't finished adding content. I made it with Bootstrap and Vue.

Here is where my music and music-related projects go. I don't really like posting this kind of stuff publicly, but I realized that I should at least have a few tracks on the internet.

I have not done much art professionally or for classes, and thus, most of the art that you'll see on this page is somewhat self-indulgent. I do my 3D modelling with Blender, and digital illustration with Adobe Illustrator. I do my video production with Premiere and motion graphics/camera tracking with After Effects.

I've been writing code and working on projects since I was introduced to TI-BASIC at age 13.
I am proficient in Python, Java, C++, JavaScipt, and HTML/CSS. I have specific experience with Java Android development, VueJS, JQuery, Bootstrap, Web2Py, Pygame, Pyhook, and other small python packages. I also have some experience with Perl, Ocaml, and Lisp, though just in the context of school.
Practical engineering is an interest of mine, and I have done a few projects with Arduino, most notably the Pedalbox controller that is featured on this page.
The code for this website and some of my public projects is available on my GitHub page.
